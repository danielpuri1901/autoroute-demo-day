04 — Voice
Brand Voice

Simple > Clever

Say it plain. No jargon, no buzzwords, no filler. If a planner wouldn't say it, we don't write it.

Direct > Vague

Lead with the point. "Saves 45 minutes a day" beats "enhances operational efficiency."

Warm > Cold

We're talking to real people doing hard work. Helpful and sharp, not robotic or corporate.

Do

  • "Your routes are ready." — direct, clear
  • "Saved you 23 km today." — specific, helpful
  • "Something went wrong. We're looking into it." — honest
  • "Routes get smarter as you use them." — simple benefit
  • "Built for planners, not IT departments." — warm, specific

Don't

  • "Leverage our AI-powered optimization engine" — buzzwords
  • "Unlock next-level logistics efficiency" — vague marketing
  • "Error 500: Internal Server Error" — cold, unhelpful
  • "Our proprietary ML model..." — nobody asked
  • "Revolutionizing the supply chain" — generic, overblown
