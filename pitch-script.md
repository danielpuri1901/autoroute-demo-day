# AutoRoute — Demo Day Script (3 min)

## Slide 1: Title
**AutoRoute — Learns which routes fit your fleet. On autopilot.**

Take a look around you. The shirt you're wearing, the pants you have on, even the chair you're sitting on — has all been handled by at least three different delivery companies.

The delivery industry is known for its tight margins. Delivery companies need to be as efficient as possible. Being late or missing one stop can lose them a customer.

At the core of their business is the route planning software. That makes or breaks their business.

---

## Slide 2: Problem
**Different operations + same software = shit routes**

There's more than just DHL and FedEx that deliver your packages. There's the small bike courier with a 20-bike fleet. There's refrigerated vans that deliver food.

Every delivery company is different in how and what they deliver. And they all use the same software.

All existing software does the same thing — it creates generic routes. But as soon as you want to tailor it to your business, it requires a lot of money and a lot of time. Development time for a specific route change reaches 4 months. And hiring an in-house team costs a fortune — something SMEs can't pay for.
